Professor Carbon

Carbon is a multidisciplinary artist whose work encompasses sound, performance, film, sculpture, and multimedia installation.

She has taught at universities including Maryland Institute College of Art, Hofstra University, The Borough of Manhattan Community College, The Fashion Institute of Technology, The New School, Ramapo College, Pratt Institute, and New York University.

Education: MPS. ITP, New York University, New York, NY BFA. Art and Technology, The School of the Art Institute of Chicago, Chicago, IL
